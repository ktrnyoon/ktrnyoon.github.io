Non-Hermitian Photonics

Exceptional Points in Photonic Structures

Designing and experimentally demonstrating exceptional points in passive waveguides and InAs/GaAs quantum dot membrane structures. Work spans COMSOL/RCWA simulation through cryogenic angle-resolved photoluminescence and Fourier-plane imaging at University of Würzburg.

Non-Hermitian Optics Quantum Dots ARPL / TRPL COMSOL · RCWA

research-comb.jpg
microcomb / ring resonator setup photo
Nonlinear Integrated Photonics

Microcombs & Frequency Conversion

Full PIC measurement platform setup for nonlinear optics and microcomb generation. Dispersion-engineered ring resonators, broadband SHG-based spectral transfer, and second-harmonic microcomb transfer in concentric ring resonators. MPW fabrication via Ligentec AN350.

Microcombs SHG Ring Resonators Ligentec AN350 GDS · gdsfactory

research-syn.jpg
2D material / device microscopy image
Optoelectronics · Neuromorphic

2D-Material Optoelectronic Synapses

Design, fabrication, and characterization of van der Waals heterostructure synaptic devices (MoS₂/InSe) for neuromorphic computing. The work demonstrated tunable positive and negative post-synaptic currents enabling highly accurate spiking neural networks. Published in Advanced Functional Materials (Front Cover).
